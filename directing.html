Voice Directing/Directing

Nicole has been able to combine her love for actors, teaching, and mentoring with the addition of Voice Director/Director to her list of accomplishments. Being able to be part of a team throughout the entire length of a project, to help shape memorable performances, and contributing to projects that are challenging cultural and gender norms are just some of the highlights of this facet of Nicole’s career path.

For a full list of credits please contact Nicole's agent Caroline Young at caroline@thecharacters.com
